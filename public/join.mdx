You can use any email address to join the mailing list, its domain does not have be [cam.ac.uk](https://www.cam.ac.uk).

On submission, you'll be redirected to the University mailing list service, [sympa](https://lists.cam.ac.uk/sympa/), to subscribe with one-click. You'll then receive a confirmation email regarding your subscription. Alternatively you can find the society's list under [`soc-aim`](https://lists.cam.ac.uk/sympa/info/soc-aim) to subscribe directly on sympa.

If you have any issues with the mailing list, contact [soc-aim-request@lists.cam.ac.uk](mailto:soc-aim-request@lists.cam.ac.uk).
